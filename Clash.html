Clash of Clans is a free-to-play strategy game that was both developed and punlished by Supercell. Originally released in 2012 for iOS it now spans various platforms including Android devices and Windows

Clash of Clans Review:

Clash of Clans is an ideal game for those who love the strategy genre. Players can find their own communities through the clan system which allows anyone and everyone from across the globe to come and work together to achieve common goals. The combat mechanics truly allow the game to shine as it has been doing sice releaase. The game features various classes that all have their own unique strengths, from Barbarian to Dragon. Users can utilise all these classes in order to create the most ideal composition to both defend their settlements and attack other users settlements. However, be warned as the composition of your army can be the difference between triumph and utter defeat. Following the strategy genre's format players are able to build bases or "settlements" in various layouts to maximise resources and defences.

All in all a truly immersive and innovative mobile now PC game with fierce competition and a leading force in the world of mobile gaming. It offers many things to it's playerbase: Community, Personalisation/ A personal identity within the realm and of course strategy
