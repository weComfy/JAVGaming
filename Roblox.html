Roblox Review

Spawning into existence in late 2006, Roblox has evolved into its own universe attracting millions of users worldwide. Roblox, the immensely popoular online game that is available across multiple platforms, ranging from Mobile and PC all the way to Meta Quest devices. The reason for said popularity is the versatile and unique experience it grants users. Players can create, play and share different games and worlds that have been created by other users. Further boosting its community and social base. The diverse range of games available within the world of Roblox caters to many and all, including those from different age and interest groups.

All in all, Roblox offers a prospourous and highly interactive landscape for players to explore various worlds, have party adventures with both large and small groups and for the creatively inclined, create and customise their in-game avatars using varying virtual items offered. The game consistently adapts and due to the strong developer interaction consistently updates which further solidifies its position in the gaming space as a leading game with ever explanding gameplay

Perks & Detriments

  • Perks:
    • Continually expanding library of games by fellow users
    • Highly sociable community with multiplayer capabilites
    • Highly customizable designs for unique avatars
    • Detriments:
    • Internet connection needed
    • Game quality can be inconsistent
    • Microtransactions may be present within some games
